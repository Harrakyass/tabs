Mao Mao

Maomao, known for her sharp mind and keen sense of deduction, is an apothecary working within the Imperial Palace of Li. With an extensive knowledge of herbs and poisons, she is often tasked with solving complex medical and mysterious issues that arise in the court. Despite her humble origins and traumatic past, she remains calm under pressure and highly curious, often finding herself embroiled in palace intrigues and conspiracies. Her pragmatic and intelligent approach makes her an invaluable asset, even as she navigates the dangers of the palace.

  • Full Name: Maomao (also known as Xiao Mao)
  • Occupation: Apothecary, Poison Tester, Lady-in-Waiting
  • Powers: Expert in herbs and poisons, keen sense of deduction, practical problem-solving skills
  • Personality: Practical, intelligent, calm under pressure, curious
  • Notable Traits: Works in the Imperial Palace of Li, uses her knowledge to solve mysteries, has a calm demeanor despite her traumatic past.

Frieren

Frieren is an elf mage who was part of the Hero Party that once defeated the Demon King. Known for her stoic and aloof demeanor, she has a carefree attitude that masks her emotional sensitivity. With her powerful mana manipulation and spellcasting abilities, she is a force to be reckoned with in combat. Frieren's journey continues even after the heroic era, as she seeks to understand human emotions and relationships better. Her longevity and elven perspective give her a unique view of the transient nature of life, making her introspective and often reflective on her past adventures.

  • Full Name: Frieren
  • Occupation: Mage of the Hero Party
  • Powers: Mana manipulation, powerful spells, exceptional combat skills
  • Personality: Stoic, aloof, carefree, emotionally sensitive, playful
  • Notable Traits: Member of the Hero Party alongside Himmel, Eisen, and Heiter, journeying to defeat the Demon King.

Kana

Kana, a determined and ambitious idol, is the daughter of the famous idol Kana Miura. With a natural talent for singing and acting, she strives to make a name for herself in the competitive entertainment industry. Her charisma and caring nature endear her to fans and colleagues alike. Despite the pressures and challenges of the idol world, Kana remains resilient, driven by her passion and the desire to live up to her mother's legacy while carving out her own identity. Her journey is marked by growth, self-discovery, and the pursuit of her dreams.

  • Full Name: Arima Kana
  • Occupation: Idol, Actress, Former genius child actress ^_^
  • Powers: Singing, acting, charisma
  • Personality: Determined, ambitious, caring
  • Notable Traits: Former genius child actress, determined to reclaim her former glory

Megumin

Megumin is an eccentric and enthusiastic wizard known for her obsession with explosion magic. As a member of the Crimson Demons, she possesses immense destructive power but can only cast one explosive spell per day, which leaves her drained and unable to fight further. Her passionate declaration, "Explosion is my passion!" epitomizes her character's single-minded dedication to this powerful but impractical magic. Despite her naivety and quirky personality, Megumin's unwavering determination and loyalty to her friends make her a beloved and indispensable part of her adventuring party.

  • Full Name: Megumin
  • Occupation:Arch Wizard
  • Powers: Explosion magic
  • Personality: ccentric, enthusiastic about explosions, somewhat naive
  • Notable Traits: Member of the Crimson Demons, known for her love of explosions and her catchphrase "Explosion is my passion!"
